Artcell

Popular band Artcell will celebrate its 20th founding anniversary performing at a concert titled '20 Years of Artcellism' on December 24. The concert will be organised by Asiatic Experiential Marketing Limited where Artcell and contemporary metal bands will entertain music lovers..

Warfaze

Popular rock band Warfaze is all set for a playback song in the film, titled Girgiti. The band will sing the title track of the movie. The film is directed by Sourav Kundu and produced by Up Studio production house and publications.

Arbovirus

In 2002, two friends and former classmates Suharto Sherif and Asif Asgar Ranjan started a band, pursuing their dreams in music. 17 years, 3 studio albums, 12 singles and an 'infection' epidemic later, Arbovirus stands tall as one of the mightiest bands in Bangladesh today.
